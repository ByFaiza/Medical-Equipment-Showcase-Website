Ultrasound Machine

Description

Ultrasound systems use high-frequency sound waves to generate real-time images of internal organs, tissues, and blood vessels. They are widely used due to their safety and versatility.

  • Radiation-free technology
  • Portable configurations
  • High image quality

Applications

  • Cardiology
  • Abdominal Examinations
  • Vascular Studies

Benefits

  • Non-invasive imaging
  • Real-time visualization
  • No ionizing radiation
