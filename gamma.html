Gamma Camera

Description

A Gamma Camera is a nuclear medicine imaging device used to detect gamma radiation emitted from radiopharmaceuticals inside the patient's body. It allows healthcare professionals to assess organ function and identify abnormalities.


Key Features

  • High-resolution imaging
  • Functional organ assessment
  • Advanced detector technology
  • Accurate diagnostic support

Applications

  • Bone Scans
  • Cardiac Imaging
  • Thyroid Studies
  • Renal Imaging
  • Oncology Diagnostics

Benefits

  • Early disease detection
  • Functional imaging capabilities
  • Improved diagnostic confidence
