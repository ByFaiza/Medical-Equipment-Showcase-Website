MRI Scanner

Description

Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) scanners use strong magnetic fields and radio waves to create highly detailed images of soft tissues and organs.

  • Non-invasive procedure
  • High diagnostic accuracy
  • Advanced imaging capabilities

Applications

  • Spine Assessment

Benefits
